Our story

Dallas's Trusted Painting Professionals Since 1998

To deliver exceptional painting services with integrity, craftsmanship, and a commitment to customer satisfaction — making every space we touch more beautiful and durable.

James House Paint Inc was founded in 1998 by James House, a Dallas native with a passion for quality craftsmanship. Starting as a small crew painting residential homes in Lakewood, James built a reputation for meticulous work, honest pricing, and reliable service. Over the years, the company expanded to serve commercial clients across the metroplex, but our core values remain the same: treat every project like it's our own home.

Today, James House Paint Inc is a family-owned business with a team of experienced painters who share James's dedication to excellence. We invest in training, use premium materials, and stand behind every job with a satisfaction guarantee. Whether it's a single room or a multi-story office building, we bring the same attention to detail and pride in our work.
