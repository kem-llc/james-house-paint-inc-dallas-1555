Our story

Over 35 Years of Painting Excellence in Dallas

To deliver beautiful, durable paint finishes with exceptional customer service, treating every home as if it were our own.

James House Paint Inc was founded in 1985 by James House, a Dallas native with a passion for craftsmanship. Starting as a one-man operation, James built a reputation for meticulous attention to detail and honest pricing. Over the decades, the company grew into a trusted name in the Dallas painting industry, serving thousands of homeowners and businesses.

Today, we remain family-owned and operated, with a dedicated team of skilled painters who share James's commitment to quality. We invest in training, premium materials, and modern techniques to ensure every project exceeds expectations. From historic homes in Lakewood to modern condos in Uptown, we treat each job with pride.
