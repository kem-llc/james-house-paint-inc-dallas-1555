Cabinet Painting

Spray-finish cabinet painting for a factory-quality look.

Schedule this service

Overview

Give your kitchen or bathroom a stunning makeover without the cost of replacement. Our cabinet painting process includes thorough degreasing, sanding, priming, and spraying with durable cabinet-grade paint for a smooth, hard finish.

What's included

  • Cost-effective alternative to replacement
  • Durable, easy-to-clean finish
  • Wide range of colors and sheens
  • Minimal downtime – typically 3-5 days

How it works

1

Removal & Cleaning

We remove doors, drawers, and hardware, then clean all surfaces with degreaser.

2

Sanding & Priming

We sand to create a smooth base, apply primer, and sand again for a flawless surface.

3

Spraying & Reassembly

We spray multiple thin coats of paint, then reinstall doors and hardware with precision alignment.

Common questions

Can you match existing cabinet colors?

Absolutely. We can color-match any sample or provide custom colors. We also offer popular shades like white, gray, and navy.

How long does cabinet paint last?

With proper prep and quality paint, cabinet finishes last 5-10 years. We use a catalyzed urethane for extra durability.
