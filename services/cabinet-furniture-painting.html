Cabinet & Furniture Painting

Give your cabinets and furniture a fresh, modern look without the cost of replacement.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our cabinet and furniture painting service revitalizes kitchens, bathrooms, and custom pieces. We use durable, low-VOC paints and a meticulous process that includes cleaning, sanding, priming, and multiple coats for a smooth, factory-like finish. Hardware removal and reinstallation included.

What's included

  • Cost-effective alternative to replacement
  • Custom color matching for any style
  • Durable finish that resists chipping and fading
  • Fast turnaround — typically 3-5 days

How it works

1

Removal & Cleaning

We remove doors, drawers, and hardware, then thoroughly clean all surfaces to remove grease and grime.

2

Sanding & Priming

We sand surfaces to promote adhesion, apply a high-quality primer, and sand again for a smooth base.

3

Painting & Reinstallation

We apply two coats of premium paint with a sprayer or brush, then reinstall hardware and doors, ensuring proper alignment.

Common questions

Can you paint laminate cabinets?

Yes, with proper preparation and a bonding primer, we can paint laminate cabinets for a durable finish.

How long does cabinet painting last?

With proper care, our cabinet paint jobs last 5-10 years or more. We use high-quality paints designed for cabinetry.
