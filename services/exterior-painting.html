Exterior Painting

Boost your home's curb appeal and protect it from the elements with our durable exterior painting solutions.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our exterior painting service includes thorough surface preparation — power washing, scraping loose paint, caulking gaps, and priming — followed by two coats of high-quality exterior paint. We use weather-resistant paints that stand up to Dallas sun and storms.

What's included

  • Enhances curb appeal and property value
  • Protects against weather, rot, and pests
  • Includes pressure washing and caulking
  • Long-lasting finishes with warranty

How it works

1

Inspection & Prep

We inspect the exterior for damage, power wash surfaces, scrape old paint, and repair wood or siding as needed.

2

Masking & Priming

We mask windows, doors, and fixtures, then apply primer to bare wood or metal for optimal adhesion.

3

Painting & Final Walkthrough

We apply two coats of premium exterior paint, then clean up and walk the property with you to ensure satisfaction.

Common questions

How often should I repaint my home's exterior?

Typically every 5-10 years depending on material and climate. We can assess your home's condition during a free estimate.

Do you paint in winter?

Yes, as long as temperatures remain above 35°F. We use paints formulated for cooler conditions when needed.
