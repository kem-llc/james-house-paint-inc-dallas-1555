Interior Painting

Expert interior painting for walls, ceilings, trim, and more.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our interior painting service is designed to minimize disruption while maximizing quality. We protect your floors and furniture, patch holes, sand smooth, and apply two coats of premium paint for a flawless finish. Choose from thousands of colors.

What's included

  • Low-VOC paints safe for family and pets
  • Sharp, clean lines without drips
  • Minimal mess with drop cloths and tape
  • Fast turnaround – most rooms done in a day

How it works

1

Consultation & Estimate

We visit your home, discuss your vision, measure the space, and provide a detailed written quote.

2

Preparation

We move furniture, cover floors, patch imperfections, and tape edges for perfect lines.

3

Painting & Cleanup

Our painters apply primer and two coats of paint, then remove all coverings and leave your home spotless.

Common questions

How long does interior paint last?

With proper preparation and quality paint, interior paint can last 5-10 years depending on wear and sunlight.

Do I need to be home during painting?

Not necessarily. We can coordinate key access or work around your schedule. Many clients provide a key and trust our crew.
