Interior Painting

Transform your home's interior with precision-rolled walls, crisp trim, and a flawless finish that stands the test of time.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our interior painting service covers every room — from bedrooms and kitchens to basements and hallways. We meticulously prepare surfaces, patch imperfections, and apply premium paint for a smooth, durable result. Color consultation is included to help you choose the perfect palette.

What's included

  • Low-VOC paints for safer indoor air quality
  • Detailed protection of floors, furniture, and fixtures
  • Expert color matching and advice
  • Fast, clean completion with minimal disruption

How it works

1

Consultation & Estimate

We meet on-site to discuss your vision, measure spaces, and provide a detailed written estimate with no hidden fees.

2

Preparation & Priming

We move furniture, cover surfaces, patch holes, sand, and apply primer to ensure perfect paint adhesion.

3

Painting & Cleanup

Our painters apply two coats of premium paint with careful cutting and rolling. We then clean up thoroughly and review the work with you.
